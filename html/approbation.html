Generate certificate

Please note that you must complete the registration form beforehand.

By clicking on "Want certificate", a check will be carried out. We will check that all the quizzes have been completed, that each mark is higher than 5/10 and that the overall mark is at least 8/10 for the certificate of completion.For the attendance certificate, a check will be made to ensure that all the courses have been read within the minimum time. If any of the criteria are not met, an error message will be issued.

By clicking on "Give points" will give you all the points you need to generate the certificate.
